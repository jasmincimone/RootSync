# Business Rules

## Platform Rules

-   Community comes before commerce.
-   Vendors are verified RootSync participants.
-   Directory Listings are view-only until claimed.
-   PostgreSQL is the source of truth.
-   Every feature must support the RootSync Constitution.

## Marketplace

-   Vendors own their products.
-   Stripe Connect handles payouts.
-   Reviews must come from legitimate interactions.

## AI

-   AI assists but does not replace human expertise.
-   Recommendations should be actionable and marketplace-aware.
