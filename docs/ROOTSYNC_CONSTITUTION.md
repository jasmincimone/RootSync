# RootSync Constitution

## Preamble

RootSync exists to strengthen local communities by making local living easier, more connected, and more accessible.

Technology should strengthen human relationships—not replace them.

This Constitution establishes the principles that guide every decision made within RootSync.

---

# Article I — Mission

**To make local living easier, more connected, and more accessible.**

---

# Article II — Vision

RootSync exists to become the digital infrastructure for local living by connecting people with local goods, services, resources, events, and community.

---

# Article III — Core Principles

## Community Before Commerce
Relationships come first. Commerce should strengthen communities.

## Local First
Prioritize local discovery, participation, and opportunity whenever possible.

## Simplicity Wins
Build simple, intuitive experiences that reduce friction.

## Trust Is Fundamental
Earn trust through transparency, verification, and consistency.

## Education Creates Confidence
Teach before selling whenever appropriate.

## Technology Should Feel Invisible
Technology should quietly enable meaningful human experiences.

---

# Article IV — The RootSync Test

Every major decision should answer **Yes** to these questions:

- Does this make local living easier?
- Does this strengthen local communities?
- Does this align with our mission?
- Can it be explained simply?

If not, reconsider the proposal.

---

# Article V — Platform Philosophy

RootSync is a platform—not simply a marketplace, social network, AI assistant, directory, or booking tool.

Every capability should work together to strengthen local ecosystems.

---

# Article VI — Governance

Major changes should generally follow:

Idea

↓

RFC (Request for Comments)

↓

PRD (Product Requirements Document)

↓

Implementation

↓

ADR (Architecture Decision Record)

↓

Release

↓

Changelog

---

# Article VII — Stewardship

Every contributor should:

- Prefer clarity over cleverness.
- Prefer simplicity over complexity.
- Build reusable platform capabilities.
- Leave the platform better than they found it.

---

# Article VIII — Living Document

The Constitution is a living document and may evolve as RootSync grows while remaining true to its mission.

---

# Closing Statement

RootSync is an investment in stronger communities.

Every line of code should help people discover, support, and participate in local living.
