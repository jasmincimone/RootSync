
# RootSync Product Bible
## Version 1.0 (Working Draft)

> **North Star:** Make local living easier, more connected, and more accessible.

---

# Why RootSync Exists

Technology has made it easier than ever to buy almost anything from almost anywhere. In the process, communities have become disconnected from the people, businesses, knowledge, and opportunities that already exist around them.

RootSync exists to rebuild that local infrastructure—not by replacing communities with technology, but by helping communities discover, support, and strengthen one another.

---

# Mission

To make local living easier, more connected, and more accessible by helping communities discover, support, and grow with one another.

---

# Vision

Create a future where supporting local is the default. Every community should have an easy way to discover local businesses, services, educators, makers, growers, events, and opportunities.

---

# Philosophy

Technology should reveal community, not replace it.

Community comes before commerce.

Teach first. Sell second.

Relationships create stronger local economies.

Simple solutions outperform complicated ones.

---

# The RootSync Principles

1. Community Before Commerce
2. Local First
3. Build for Simplicity
4. Trust is Everything
5. Education Creates Confidence
6. Technology Should Feel Invisible

# What RootSync Is

A platform connecting people with locally produced goods, services, knowledge, events, and community.

---

# What RootSync Is Not

- Just another marketplace
- Just another social network
- Just another AI chatbot
- Just another directory

RootSync combines these into one connected local ecosystem.

---

# Members

- Strengthen local communities.
- Solve a real problem.
