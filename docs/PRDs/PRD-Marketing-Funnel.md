# PRD: Marketing Funnel

## Goal

Convert visitors into long-term community members.

## Flow

Landing Page → Email Capture → Welcome Email → Product Offers →
Consultation → RootSync Community

## Requirements

-   Email opt-in
-   PostgreSQL as source of truth
-   Sync with email provider
-   QR code support
-   Segmentation by interest

## Metrics

-   Conversion rate
-   Open rate
-   Consultation bookings
